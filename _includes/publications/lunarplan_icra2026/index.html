Energy-Constrained Navigation for Planetary Rovers under Hybrid RTG-Solar Power

Under review for an international robotics conference

Tianxin Hu*, Weixiang Guo*, Ruimeng Liu, Xinhang Xu, Rui Qian, Jinyu Chen, Shenghai Yuan, Lihua Xie

We present STARC, a see-through augmented reality framework for human-robot collaboration that fuses responder-mounted and robot-mounted LiDAR sensing. The system enables real-time visualization of occluded humans and hazards, enhancing situational awareness and safety in emergency response missions.
