SplatSSC: Decoupled Depth-Guided Gaussian Splatting for 3D Semantic Scene Completion

Under review, 2025

Rui Qian*, Haozhi Cao*, Tianchen Deng, Shenghai Yuan, Lihua Xie

We introduce SplatSSC, a decoupled depth-guided Gaussian splatting framework that leverages depth-aware initialization and geometry-semantic disentangled aggregation to achieve more efficient and accurate 3D semantic scene completion.
