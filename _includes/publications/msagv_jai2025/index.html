Tire wear aware trajectory tracking control for Multi-axle Swerve-drive Autonomous Mobile Robots

Accepted by Journal of Automation and Intelligence (JAI) , June 2025

Tianxin Hu*, Xinhang Xu*, Thien-Minh Nguyen, Fen Liu, Shenghai Yuan, Lihua Xie

We present a hierarchical model predictive control framework for multi-axle swerve-drive robots that integrates tire-wear modeling into the optimization objective, achieving up to 65% reduction in tire wear while maintaining high trajectory-tracking accuracy.
